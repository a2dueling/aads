Getting Started

Want to compete?

To compete in USA Fencing tournaments, you'll need a current USA Fencing membership. Student and adult membership options are available at a range of price points, and your membership covers you for a full season of competition.

USA Fencing membership

Annual membership can be purchased directly through USA Fencing at usafencing.org. Members get access to the national rating system, insurance coverage at sanctioned events, and the ability to register for rated tournaments.

Tournament registration

Most regional tournaments are listed and managed through AskFRED. Create a free account, find an event you want to enter, and register online. Tournament fees vary by event.
